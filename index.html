Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CosmiCon 2025, including eligibility, registration, quiz format, and more.

  • Who is eligible to participate in CosmiCon 2025?

    CosmiCon 2025 is open to all university and college students in Pakistan. Students from any academic year or discipline can participate. Participants will compete while representing their university, city, and province.

  • What is the registration fee, and how do I pay it?

    The registration fee is PKR 500 per participant. It is mandatory to secure your quiz slot.

    Payment Process:

    1. Fill out the registration form.
    2. After filling out the form, payment details will be provided for fee submission.
    3. Make the payment through the provided method.
    4. Once the payment is successfully made, you will receive a confirmation email with further details regarding the quiz.

    Note: If the registration fee is not submitted, your quiz slot will not be confirmed.

  • What is the format of the quiz, and how long will it last?

    The quiz is an online MCQ-based test consisting of 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

    Quiz Details:

    • Total Duration: 2 hours (120 minutes)
    • Monitoring: The quiz will be AI-monitored, ensuring fair play.
    • Randomized Questions: Questions will be randomly selected from the syllabus.
    • This ensures a fair and competitive test for all participants.
  • What is the syllabus for the quiz?

    The quiz consists of two main sections:

    1. Technical Section (50 MCQs)

    Questions will be taken from CosmiCode eBooks, covering:

    • C++ Programming
    • Python Programming

    Each eBook contains 50 MCQs, and questions will be randomly selected from them.

    2. General AI Knowledge (50 MCQs)

    This section will cover:

    • Introduction to AI
    • AI Applications in Real Life
    • AI‚Äôs Impact on Jobs & Society

    These questions will be non-technical and based on general awareness of Artificial Intelligence.

  • Can I choose my quiz time slot?

    Yes, participants can select their preferred time slot during registration. If a slot is full, they will be asked to choose another available slot.

  • How will I receive the quiz link?

    The quiz link will be sent to the registered email address before the selected time slot. Participants must check their inbox and spam folder.

  • What anti-cheating measures are in place?

    The quiz is AI-monitored with tab-switching restrictions, live proctoring, and auto disqualification for any detected cheating.

  • What happens if I face technical issues during the quiz?

    Important Guidelines for Participants

    Participants must ensure:

    • Stable Internet Connection
    • Fully Charged Device (Laptop, PC, or Mobile)
    • Quiet Environment
